# Annual Review SOP

## 1. Purpose

To capture the process for employees, Tech Leads, and Program Managers to administer Annual Reviews in Paylocity, ensuring consistent performance evaluation, documentation, and compensation discussions on an annual basis.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all Oddball employees, supervisors/reviewers, Program Managers (PgMs), and Tech Leads. Impacts the full employee population across all projects.

## 3. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
| ------ | ------ |
| Employee | Completes self-evaluation in Paylocity, updates Oddball Resume & Skills Matrix and submits to Nicola White, completes annual anti-harassment training, signs off on completed review |
| Program Manager (PgM) / Supervisor | Completes manager review in Paylocity, schedules ~1 hour Zoom with employee, shares raise information, signs off on completed review, updates master QC/Annual tracker after review |
| Tech Lead | May serve as supervisor for engineers on their team; includes PgM in review meeting so PgM can add comments and observations |

## 4. Procedure

**Employee:**

1. Log into Paylocity — find "Oddball Performance Review" under Tasks to Complete, or navigate via hamburger menu > Performance > My Reviews > Active Review
2. Complete Core Values section (rate each value, comments optional) — click Save before moving on
3. Complete Right People In The Right Seat section (yes/no, comments optional)
4. Complete Employee Comments section (required)
5. Skip Supervisor Comments section — this is for the reviewer
6. Add supporting documentation if applicable
7. Click orange "Submit to Reviewer" button — do not click this until all sections are complete or the review will be locked
8. Sign off on completed review in Paylocity after the Zoom meeting
9. Update Oddball Resume & Skills Matrix and submit to Nicola White
10. Complete annual anti-harassment training through Paylocity

**Supervisor/Reviewer:**

1. Log into Paylocity > hamburger menu > Performance > Reviews > Active Reviews
2. Select the review via the "Oddball Performance Review" link
3. Complete Core Values section (comments optional) — click Save, do not click "Make Visible to Employee" until all sections are complete
4. Complete Right People In The Right Seat section (yes/no, comments optional)
5. Review Employee Comments section
6. Complete Supervisor Comments section (required)
7. Add supporting documentation if applicable
8. Schedule ~1 hour Zoom with employee (and PgM if Tech Lead is running the review)
9. Click "Make Visible to Employee" before or after the Zoom — interchangeable depending on comfort level
10. Sign off on completed review in Paylocity

## 5. Supporting Processes and Historical Context

- Annual reviews are conducted in the quarter and month the employee started at Oddball, replacing the regular QC for that timeframe
- Annual reviews must be completed on or before the employee's work anniversary date
- Annual Raise SOP — TBD

## 6. Considerations

- **Timeline:** Employees have 2 weeks to complete their self-evaluation from the date it is assigned in Paylocity; supervisors have an additional 2 weeks; due on the first day of the pay period following the employee's work anniversary
- Paylocity will send the employee a link to complete their Annual Review — it may be necessary to remind employees to complete and submit so the reviewer receives their Paylocity notification
- Do NOT click "Submit to Reviewer" until all sections are complete — doing so locks the review from further edits; contact Nicola White to unlock
- Do NOT click "Make Visible to Employee" until all sections are complete — doing so locks the review; contact Nicola White to unlock
- PgM shares raise information with the employee either during the Annual Review or separately if shadowing a Tech Lead review

## 7. Tools and Formats

- **Paylocity** — primary platform for completing and documenting Annual Reviews
- **Zoom** — used for the ~1 hour review meeting between employee and supervisor
- **Oddball Resume & Skills Matrix** — updated by employee as part of the annual review process
- **QC/Annual Tracker** (Google Sheet) — updated by PgM after the review is completed

## 8. Additional Notes

- If a review is accidentally submitted or made visible before completion, contact **Nicola White** to unlock it

## 10. Deviations

- In the quarter when an annual review is held, the QC is skipped — log "Annual" in the QC/Annual tracker in place of a date
- New employees may have fewer QCs during their first calendar year depending on their start date
