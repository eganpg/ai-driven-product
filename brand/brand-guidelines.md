# Brand Guidelines

This document defines the company brand standards applied by the `brand-apply` skill.
Update each section as the brand evolves. Example files are in `brand/examples/`.

---

## Typography

- **Document title font:** Source Sans Regular, centered (cover page) — via Google Fonts
- **Body font:** Source Sans, regular weight — via Google Fonts
- **Section headings (H1):** Bold, brand teal color
- **Sub-headings (H2):** Bold, black
- **Table cell text:** Italic
- **Footer text:** Bold, black ("Level 2 Confidential")
- **Line spacing:** TBD
- **Notes:** Title on cover page should be prominently sized and centered. Internal page headings use teal to match brand color.

---

## Colors

- **Primary (brand teal):** Used for logo, section headings, horizontal rule under logo — exact hex TBD (approx. #3ECFB2 or similar)
- **Secondary:** Black — used for body text, sub-headings, footer
- **Background:** White
- **Text color:** Black
- **Notes:** Teal is used sparingly — headings and logo accent only. Confirm exact hex from brand team.

---

## Logo

- **Logo mark:** Circular "O" mark + "Oddball" wordmark + tagline "A strangely human digital agency"
- **Cover page placement:** Centered, upper third of page
- **Internal page placement:** Top left, followed by a full-width teal horizontal rule beneath it
- **Clear space rules:** TBD
- **Approved variations:** TBD
- **Notes:** Tagline ("A strangely human digital agency") appears on cover page logo only, not on internal pages.

---

## Header & Footer

- **Cover page header:** Logo centered, upper third
- **Cover page footer:** Company address bottom right — Oddball, 1775 Tysons Blvd., 5th Floor, Tysons, VA 22102
- **Internal page header:** Oddball logo top left + full-width teal horizontal rule spanning page width
- **Internal page footer:** "Level 2 Confidential" bold, bottom left (or "[Level 2 Confidential]" depending on document status)
- **Notes:** Every page including cover should carry the confidentiality classification in the footer.

---

## Document Structure

- **Cover page elements (in order):**
  1. Logo centered (with tagline)
  2. Document title centered (large serif, middle of page)
  3. Company address bottom right
  4. Version History table at bottom

- **Version History table columns:** Version Number | Implemented By | Revision Date | Approved By | Approval Date | Description of Change

- **Internal pages:** Logo + teal rule at top, body content, confidentiality footer

---

## General Rules

- Every document must include a Version History table on the cover or second page
- Every page must carry a confidentiality classification in the footer
- Teal is reserved for the logo, headings, and the header rule — do not use for body text
- Document titles use sentence case or title case consistently throughout
